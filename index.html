Our Work

Wake up to the sound of birds greeting the day, and free range eggs for breakfast. Are you an animal lover. We have chickens, ducks & 2 dogs.

We are only a short 5 min walk to the Railway station or bus stop and 15mins from the CBD, and Wellington's vast Café society. Take a stroll along Wellington's water front and enjoy the amazing views and Te Papa Museum.

If you love the beach. We are only 10mins drive from 3 different beaches, where you can swim, surfer or paddle board. Or just walk along the many local walking tracks along the shore or up the hills, and enjoy the splendor that is Wellington.
